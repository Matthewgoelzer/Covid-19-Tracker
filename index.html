This website is a tool to visualize Covid-19 data for the United States of America.

The first section represents data for the entire United States. To look at a specific state, select a state
on the map and that state's data will be located below.

The data is updated daily by 5pm PST.

This website is under constant updating. Therefore, the layout and graphs may change over time.
